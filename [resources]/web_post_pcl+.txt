Hello everybody! The community-made mod "Pocket Crystal League +" with expanded content has been released!

PCL+ is a community-made Pocket Crystal League mod/expansion created by Hyperion_21, Tukurai, DarkAnarchist, and 2fast4you98, that adds pokemon from Generations 3-7.
Disclaimer: the mod is still in a very early state, and will most likely be unbalanced! Make sure to report any bugs you find in the Discord Server!

- GitHub: https://github.com/Hyperion-21/pokecards_plus
- Pocket Crystal Discord: https://discord.gg/PEUqB45Hmy

And don't worry, the save files from Vanilla are completely separate, so feel free to check it out!
